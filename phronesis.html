Service Policies & Repair Risks

Electronic repairs are inherently unpredictable. Your device may be beyond repair or suffer permanent damage during the repair process, potentially rendering it unusable. This risk is higher for devices with liquid damage or those with glued-on screens (e.g., iPads), where components can fail or screens may crack during disassembly. Features like Touch ID/Face ID on Apple devices or integrated GPUs on older laptops may also fail during repair, though this is uncommon.

Estimates

Our estimates are thorough but not guaranteed to be all-inclusive or final, as some issues may only become apparent during the repair. If an estimate needs to be updated, we will inform you immediately, and all changes must be approved by you before we proceed. It is the customer's responsibility to seek an estimate; by submitting a device, you acknowledge you have performed due diligence regarding pricing.

Payment Options

We accept payments via **Cash, M-Pesa Paybill, or M-Pesa Till**. All payments must be receipted. Customers seeking refunds or warranty claims must present the original receipt. In case of a lost receipt, we reserve the right to deny the customer the device until ownership can be proven otherwise.

Limited Shop Warranty

We offer a 60-day warranty on all repairs and parts used, covering recurrence of the original issue. This warranty does not cover new issues unrelated to the original service or problems arising from user error, negligence, or physical tampering (e.g., drops, liquid damage, unauthorized modification, use of non-original charging cables). Installing new hardware or modifying existing hardware in the 60 day guarantee period also voids our warranty. Please note that our services may void your manufacturer's warranty, for which we are not liable.

If your device malfunctions due to the original repair within 60 days, we will re-perform the service at no extra cost. Refunds are only issued if we cannot fix the device after a re-attempt, minus a repair attempt fee and return shipping. You are responsible for shipping the device back for warranty repairs, and we will cover return shipping.

Estimate and Repair Time

While we strive for quick repairs (often 1-48 hours), repair times are estimates and not guaranteed due to factors beyond our control. Any stated times are based on past experience and do not represent exact timeframes or guarantees.

Replacement Parts Procedure

If parts are needed, we will inform you of the cost and estimated arrival time. A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the part cost is required if you wish to hold your device until parts arrive. We generally do not use customer-supplied parts as we cannot verify their functionality or compatibility; using your own parts voids our warranty and you waive liability for any damage caused by such parts.

Repair Attempt Fee

A repair attempt fee ranging from approximately KES 100 - KES 1,000 will be charged for most motherboard repairs, data recovery attempts, or devices with prior repair attempts, even if unsuccessful. This fee covers the time spent on your device.

Shipping Policy

We follow your provided shipping instructions. Failure to request insurance means you are solely responsible for shipping damages, and we assume no liability. Devices shipped in generic boxes will be returned in generic boxes at your own risk. Order amounts must be equal to or greater than the shipping amount; otherwise, the order may be canceled.

Data Security & Recovery

We do not guarantee the security or safety of your data. While we take precautions, we are not liable for any lost data. If a procedure may result in data loss, we will inform you and offer data backup at an additional cost. We strongly encourage you to back up all important files before service. Data recovery for SSDs/HDDs costs approximately KES 2,000 – KES 10,000, and recovered data will be placed on a new drive provided by us. Old drives are dismantled for recovery and will not be returned.

Pickup Time Limit and Storage

Devices will be held for up to 60 days after we contact you for pickup. After 60 days, devices are considered abandoned, and we reserve the right to sell or recycle them to recover service costs. If arrangements are made for pickup after 60 days, a storage fee of approximately KES 5,200 per month will apply.
